Chicken Burrito

Description

These chicken burritos will have your mouth watering like no tomorrow. They are filled with juicy chicken, crispy lettuce, melty cheese, and scrumptious pinto beans. This recipe is very easy to make and does not take much time at all to prepare. You can even bake them at the end to add a nice crunch!

Ingredients

  • 1lb of Chicken Thighs
  • 2 tblspoons of salt
  • 3/4 cup of Adobo seasoning
  • 1 12oz can of pinto beans
  • 1/2 head of iceberg lettuce
  • 2 cups of shredded pepperjack cheese
  • 1 tblspoon of olive oil
  • 1 cup of water
  • 3-4 large tortillas

Steps

  1. Trim any fat off the chicken and pat the chicken dry. Then season it with salt and let it permeate for 5 minutes.
  2. While the chicken is sitting, chop up the head of lettuce in thin inch long strips
  3. Pat the chicken dry again and trim any fat off. Then add the adobo seasoning
  4. Put a large pan on medium-high heat and put in your olive oil and coat the pan
  5. Once the pan is hot enough, place chicken thighs in pan with some space in between. Let cook on one side for 4 minutes, then flip and let cook for an additional 3 minutes.
  6. Place chicken aside on a plate, then drain your beans and add to a small pot with any seasonings you may want. Add your cup of water and bring it to a boil, once boiling bring down to a simmer and let simmer for 5-6 minutes.
  7. After beans are done, drain and let cool. While cooling, you can heat up your tortillas in the pan used for the chicken on medium heat. Remove once tortillas are warm.
  8. Finally, you can put all your ingredients together on the tortilla and wrap your burritos!
